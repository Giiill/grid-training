Fighting swords have been in the service of people for thousands of years. These weapons have been used by warriors since the fifth century AD, since the invention of the Germanic spatha, the ancestor of all European swords. Since then, constantly transforming, forged swords have firmly entered the arsenals of all medieval armies along with spears and axes, slowly giving way to swords only in the Renaissance. Variety of types and designs Centuries of improvement, together with the armor opposing the sword, have given rise to many different types and types of swords, both in terms of damage and shape. And, given the huge symbolic significance of the sword for those epochs, their utilitarian functionality of a formidable weapon goes hand in hand with the aesthetic side – rich and careful decoration.
